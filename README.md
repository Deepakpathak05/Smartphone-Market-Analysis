# 📖 Project Overview

This project demonstrates an **end-to-end Data Analytics workflow** by collecting smartphone data directly from the **Smartprix** website using **Selenium** and **BeautifulSoup**, followed by data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and the development of an interactive **Power BI Dashboard**.

The objective is to analyze smartphone market trends, pricing strategies, brand distribution, processor adoption, hardware specifications, and derive actionable business insights through interactive visualizations.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

- Automate smartphone data collection from Smartprix
- Clean and preprocess raw scraped data
- Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Discover pricing patterns and market trends
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard
- Generate business insights and recommendations

# 💡 Key Insights

- The smartphone market is dominated by a few major brands with extensive product portfolios.
- Mid-range smartphones represent the largest share of available models.
- Snapdragon and MediaTek processors dominate the market.
- Smartphones with higher RAM and storage configurations generally belong to higher price segments.
- 5G has become a standard feature across many smartphones.
- Fast charging support is increasingly common in mid-range and premium devices.

---

# 📌 Business Recommendations

- Expand product offerings within the growing mid-range segment.
- Increase availability of affordable 5G smartphones.
- Focus on larger battery capacity and fast charging to improve product competitiveness.
- Optimize pricing strategies based on hardware specifications and market positioning.
